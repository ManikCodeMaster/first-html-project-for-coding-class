Here's a simple filter coffee recipe :

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of ground coffee
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
Visit the link to buy the ingredients

Preparation

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Put coffee in a Coffee maker.
  3. When the water is ready, pour it gently over the coffee. Wait a little for it to turn into yummy coffee.
  4. In another cup, add sugar and milk. Mix them together until it's sweet and milky, just how you like it.
  5. Take the coffee you made and pour it into the sweet milky cup. Stir it well.
  6. Wait for a bit until it's not too hot to drink.
  7. Enjoy your tasty coffee with a snack or cookie!
